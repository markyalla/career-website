Recovery Guidelines
Rest & Sleep
  • Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep when possible
  • Take short naps during the day
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Limit screen time before bed
Nutrition
  • Eat regular, balanced meals
  • Stay well hydrated
  • Include omega-3 rich foods
  • Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables
Physical Recovery
  • Use heat therapy for sore muscles
  • Apply ice for inflammation
  • Try gentle massage
  • Take gentle walks daily
Emotional Recovery
  • Connect with other mothers
  • Practice mindful reading
  • Listen to calming music
  • Talk about your feelings

Daily Recovery Routine Suggestions
Morning (7:00 AM - 9:00 AM)

Gentle stretching, hydration, nutritious breakfast, and deep breathing exercises.

Midday (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM)

Short walk, healthy lunch, 10-15 minute power nap if possible.

Evening (6:00 PM - 8:00 PM)

Light exercise or yoga, warm bath, journaling, prepare for restful sleep.

Important Reminders
Medical Advice

Always consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new exercise or recovery routine, especially during pregnancy and postpartum periods.

Listen to Your Body

Every mother's journey is unique. What works for others may not work for you, and that's perfectly okay.
