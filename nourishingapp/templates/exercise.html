Exercise Tips for Mothers

  • Start slowly and listen to your body
  • Focus on low-impact activities
  • Stay hydrated and avoid overheating
  • Strengthen your pelvic floor
  • Maintain good posture exercises

  • Wait for doctor's clearance (usually 6-8 weeks)
  • Start with gentle walks
  • Focus on core and pelvic floor rehabilitation
  • Gradually increase intensity
  • Be patient with your body's recovery

  • Aim for 150 minutes of moderate activity per week
  • Include strength training 2-3 times per week
  • Exercise with your children when possible
  • Find activities you enjoy
  • Consistency is better than intensity
